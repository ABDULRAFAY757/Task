Computer Science

The Computer Science department at University offers a range of undergraduate and graduate programs in computer science and related fields. Our faculty includes experts in artificial intelligence, computer networks, cybersecurity, data science, and software engineering.

Faculty

  • Dr. Muhammad Talha

    Muhammad Talha is a Professor of Computer Science at University. He received his PhD from MIT and his research interests include natural language processing, machine learning, and computer vision.

  • Prof. Sabeeh Ahmed

    Prof. Sabeeh Ahmed is an Associate Professor of Computer Science at University. She received her PhD from Stanford and her research interests include computer graphics, virtual reality, and human-computer interaction.

  • Prof. Amjad

    Prof. Amjad is an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at University. He received his PhD from Carnegie Mellon and his research interests include distributed systems, cloud computing, and software engineering.
